What to Expect at a FREE Dinner Show

The concept behind the Dinner Show is to allow you to personally experience for yourself, in the comfort of your own home, surrounded by family and friends just how well the Red 5/Saladmaster system works.

Along with an informative presentation about important Health and Nutrition issues, guests will receive a cooking demonstration, all guests will have a delicious and tasty five course meal prepared and cooked before their eyes in around 35 minutes.

Saladmaster is a health and nutrition company from America that has been trading since 1946 and currently trades in over 40 different countries. The purpose of the dinner show is to show how to prepare and cook food using no fats, grease or oils of any kind, very little water and on very low temperatures which retains up to 93% of the valuable nutrients and minerals within the food.

Most people ask ‘Well how do you cook vegetables without water? Which is a good question because nearly everyone has been conditioned to believe that is the only way to cook vegetables. The big question that needs to be asked is WHY it's important to cook without water? The best way to explain everything is to demonstrate exactly how it all works.

The demonstration will be conducted as follows:

  1. We will take you through some seemingly minor health and nutrition tips that however have a great impact on our health
  2. We will freshly prepare and cook you and your
  3. 10 item Harvest salad drenched in lemon juice
  4. Golden brown fried chicken with our home made onion, ginger & apple source
  5. A 5 vegetable medley cooked in one container using zero water
  6. Cheesy scalloped potatoes but we de-starch the potatoes and defat the cheese.
  7. Bake you a delicious chocolate cake with a twist

All that healthy food and it's only 600 calories!! So you can eat as much as you want and still normalize your weight. (NB: You can cook any cuisine- this is just for the show).

We then conduct two scientific tests to determine whether loss of nutrients and pollution are prevalent in your current food preparation methods.

We show you the range of Saladmaster products that are available for investment as may be prioritized

Please inform us of an appropriate date at the earliest for a full demonstration to last about 2.5 hours.
